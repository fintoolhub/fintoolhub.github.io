About SIP Calculator

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a method of investing a fixed sum regularly in mutual funds. It helps in rupee cost averaging and compounding returns over the long term.

Formula Used
FV = P × [(1+r)ⁿ - 1] × (1+r)/r
Variables
P = Monthly Investment
r = Monthly Return Rate
n = Total Months
