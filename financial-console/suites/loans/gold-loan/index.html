How Jewel Loans Work

Jewel loans are secured loans where gold or silver ornaments are pledged as collateral. Banks typically offer up to 75-80% of the market value (LTV) at competitive interest rates.

Mode 1: Loan → Gold
Enter your required loan amount to find out how much gold you need to pledge based on current market rates and LTV.
Mode 2: Gold → Loan
Enter your available gold weight to calculate the maximum loan amount you can get against it.
