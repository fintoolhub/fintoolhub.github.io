Home Loan Information

EMI Formula

EMI = [P × r × (1+r)ⁿ] / [(1+r)ⁿ - 1]
Where:
P = Loan Amount
r = Monthly Interest Rate
n = Number of Months

Key Terms

  • LTV Ratio: Loan-to-Value ratio
  • Processing Fee: 0.5-1% of loan amount
  • Prepayment Charges: 0-2% of prepaid amount
  • Tax Benefits: Up to ₹2 lakh under Section 24

Tips

  • Maintain credit score above 750
  • Compare interest rates from multiple banks
  • Consider shorter tenure for less interest
  • Check for prepayment flexibility
