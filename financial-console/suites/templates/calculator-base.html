About

Formula

A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt)
Where:
A = Final amount
P = Principal amount
r = Annual interest rate
n = Compounding frequency
t = Time in years

Tips

  • Higher interest rates yield better returns
  • Longer duration increases compounding effect
  • Regular contributions boost final amount
  • Consider tax implications on returns
