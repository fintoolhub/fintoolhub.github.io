      About Monthly Planning

      A comprehensive cash flow planner helps you allocate every unit of currency to income, expenses, savings, and debt. This tool generates professional-grade statements for tracking and reporting.

      Planning Framework

      Net Cash Flow = Total Income − (Fixed + Variable + Debt + One-Time Expenses)

      Allocate remaining balance to Savings/Investments to maintain a zero-based budget.

      Key Concepts

      • 50/30/20 Rule: 50% Needs, 30% Wants, 20% Savings/Debt
      • Emergency Fund: 3–6 months of essential expenses in liquid form
      • Zero-Based Budgeting: Income − Expenses = 0 (every unit assigned)

      Tips

      • Review and adjust variable expenses weekly
      • Automate savings & SIPs on payday
      • Use CSV export for reconciliation in accounting software
