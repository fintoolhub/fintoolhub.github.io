About Stock Average Calculator

The Stock Average Calculator helps you determine your average purchase price when buying the same stock at different prices. This is essential for tracking your cost basis and making informed decisions about buying more or selling.

Formula Used
Average = (Q₁×P₁ + Q₂×P₂) ÷ (Q₁ + Q₂)
Variables
Q = Quantity of shares
P = Price per share
Average = Weighted average price
